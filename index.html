Noopept is a synthetic nootropic compound that was first introduced as a treatment for cognitive impairments and neurodegenerative diseases such as Alzheimer's and Parkinson's disease. The pros and cons of Racetams

Racetams are a family of nootropic compounds that have been shown to improve cognitive function, memory, and focus. The mechanism of action of Racetams is not well understood and their long-term effects are not fully known. Racetams are not regulated by the FDA and their quality and purity may vary between different suppliers.
