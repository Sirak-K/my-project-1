Have you heard of Noopept?

Noopept is a synthetic nootropic compound that was first introduced as a treatment for cognitive impairments and neurodegenerative diseases such as Alzheimer's and Parkinson's disease.
Noopept has gained popularity as a cognitive enhancer and is widely used for its purported memory-boosting and mood-enhancing effects.

What is Alpha-GPC?

Did you know that Alpha-GPC can be found in the brain? This is a natural choline compound that can be found in the brain and is also available as a dietary supplement. Studies have shown that Alpha-GPC supplementation can improve cognitive function and memory in both young and older adults, and may also have neuroprotective effects.

The pros and cons of Racetams

Racetams are a family of nootropic compounds that have been shown to improve cognitive function, memory, and focus. The mechanism of action of Racetams is not well understood and their long-term effects are not fully known. Racetams are not regulated by the FDA and their quality and purity may vary between different suppliers.
